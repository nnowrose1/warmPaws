Project Name: WarmPaws

Purpose: This is a platform designed for the pet owners to ensure their pets stay warm, healthy and happy during the cold winter days. In this platform, the owners can explore various pet care services those they can avail, winter care tips for pets, list of essentials that the pets need and love, and tips from experts in this field. The pet owners can find all the information in this platform to keep their beloved pets warm and healthy during the cozy winter season.

Live URL: https://ephemeral-sunflower-60d8e6.netlify.app/

Key features:
1. People will find our pet care services that we provide for pets in winter to keep them warm, healthy and happy. By registering with us, they can see the details of our services. They can register with email and password, with even their google account to our website. They can even book a service online by logging into their account. 
2. They can also explore different tips of winter care for their pets.
3. They can find the winter essentials for their pets to ensure their comfort.
4. They can find skilled vets who are there to help them with any pets related problems.
